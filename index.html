About Me

Quality isn't just what we do — it's how we think. Quality isn't just what we do — it's how we think. After almost 20 years in engineering, I've learned that the best systems aren't just built—they're cultivated. They require teams who care, leaders who listen, and a culture where everyone owns the outcome. That's the kind of environment I create.
I'm a Senior Quality Engineering and Operations leader who has spent over 8 years leading more than 100 globally distributed engineers across the US, EU, and Asia. My track record? Scaling organisations 5-10x while maintaining 95% retention, achieving 99.9%+ uptime during complex monolith-to-microservices transformations, and reducing incidents by 70% through observability, automation, and SRE practices.

The numbers tell part of the story: accelerating time-to-market by 30%, supporting FedRAMP compliance that opened $100M+ pipelines, and achieving DORA Elite metrics. Introduction the "Quality Evangelist" role — embedding quality directly into development teams, proving that quality is a mindset, not a department.
Technology should create a meaningful impact. Whether it's sustainability, healthcare, education, or social good — I'm looking for my next leadership role where technical excellence meets mission-driven work. I'm also drawn to initiatives that advance animal welfare and wellbeing—because the way we treat the most vulnerable reflects our values.

Currently seeking: Engineering, Engineering Quality or Technical Operations leadership positions where I can drive both technical transformation and positive global impact.
